The Arctic project

Take a journey to the last truly great wilderness regions - The Arctic

The project behind Arctic is a new way to get connected to a hidden world. Isolated by frozen seas and extreme weather, the Arctic supports animals found nowhere else. The Arctic Project team takes us under the ice, into the heart of glaciersand inside volcanic ice-crystal. Now that the polar worlds are melting, changing forever the lives of the animals there and, indeed, the nature of the whole planet.


This project is supposed to reach attention to a world which we lost connection to. This project wants to connect two worlds that couldn't be more apart from each other. In spite of the distance we are still connected in the environmental cycles and always will be.
